DIPLOMA

I pursue my study in Diploma where I got Diploma in Business Management in UiTM Kampus Machang.

As a pure science student, it help me to excel in my study where I able to maintain my CGPA 3.8 until

semester 5. Hardship pay off when I finally gave 'selempang pink' to my family and got Vice Canselor Awards ;)

I learnt a lot of things during my diploma live, meet a lot of people with various attitudes,
